# Data Privacy and Responsible Use Note

This portfolio project uses 100% synthetic data created solely for demonstration.

It contains no real:

- employee, applicant, student, instructor, colleague, customer, or client records;
- employer, vendor, university, or project performance data;
- email addresses, phone numbers, credentials, tokens, account IDs, or cloud resource IDs;
- confidential documents, private filesystem paths, or proprietary business rules.

Names, identifiers, organizations, conversations, operational values, and results are fictional. Any resemblance
to real people or entities is coincidental. The project should be evaluated as evidence of methodology and
technical communication, not as a statement about any real organization or individual.
